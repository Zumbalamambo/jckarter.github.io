A new toy: Strawman Comics

March 3, 2009

Next time someone makes a ridiculous argument on the Internet, make them feel bad with a punchy Strawman Comic. Point your browser at this handy form and fill it out, or check out some examples to get your imagination going:

  • BeOS
  • OS/2
  • Arc, the programming language
  • Factor, the programming language
  • Solid-state drives
  • Compiz

Strawman Comics work well with tinyurl for when you don't want to give away your punchline in your message.
