Preparing Factor for Google Summer of Code

February 25, 2010

Google's yearly Summer of Code will be taking submissions starting March 8. This year, Factor's going to be ready and waiting. We're collecting ideas for projects on our wiki. Whether you're a starry-eyed student who thinks Factor would be really cool if it just had that one feature or a world-weary expert willing to mentor someone through a dream project of yours, I invite you to throw your own ideas up on the wiki.
